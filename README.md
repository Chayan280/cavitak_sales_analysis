## Key Sales Insights
The analysis indicates strong overall performance, driven primarily by a single top-selling
product.
## Performance Overview (KPIs)
• Total Sales are significant at 40M.
• Total Profit stands at 3.81M.
• Total Orders are at 12K, suggesting a high average order value given the sales
amount.
## Sales Distribution & Trends
• Top-Performing Product: The product Desktop is the undisputed top seller,
generating 2.5 Lac in sales.
• Sales Over Time: The line chart shows a strong positive sales trend throughout the
year, with a noticeable peak in sales occurring around August. This suggests a
successful build-up towards the middle of the year, potentially due to seasonal
factors or promotions.
• Category Performance: While the data is highly skewed towards the Desktop
product, further analysis should investigate the performance of other products like
Keyboard and Headphones to ensure a diversified sales strategy.
